Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

How far in advance should I book?

+
We recommend booking at least 3–6 months before your wedding date. Spring and fall are our busiest seasons across Texas, and popular dates fill up fast — especially for baraat and estate weddings. Reach out as soon as your date is set.

What areas of Texas do you service?

+
We travel all across the great state of Texas! Our primary service areas include San Antonio, Houston, Austin, Dallas-Fort Worth, and the Hill Country (Fredericksburg, Boerne, Kerrville). We also serve South Texas — including the Rio Grande Valley, McAllen, Edinburg, and South Padre Island — as well as West Texas and everywhere in between. No corner of Texas is too far. Contact us with your venue location and we'll confirm availability and any travel fees.

Can we customize the decorations?

+
Yes! We offer customizable florals, ribbons, and fabric draping to match your wedding palette. For baraats and vidaais, we can incorporate traditional marigold garlands, red draping, and other meaningful touches. Share your vision and we'll bring it to life.

What about bad weather on our wedding day?

+
We monitor forecasts closely in the days leading up to your wedding and work with you on contingency plans if needed. Light rain is generally manageable. The safety of our horses, your guests, and our team always comes first, and we'll communicate openly every step of the way.

Do you specialize in baraat & vidaai?

+
Yes — this is one of our true specialties. Our horses are calm in high-energy crowd settings with music and dancing, and our team works closely with your coordinator to make sure every moment flows perfectly. We understand and honor the significance of both the baraat arrival and the vidaai farewell.

Are you available for events other than weddings?

+
Weddings are our primary focus and passion. That said, we do consider select bookings for other special occasions.
Looking for something other than a wedding? We also provide carriages for quinceañeras, memorial & funeral services, holiday parades, and corporate events — on a limited basis. Reach out and we'll let you know what's possible.
